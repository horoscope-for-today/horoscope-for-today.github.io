Common Questions

Horoscope FAQ

Answers to frequently asked questions about daily horoscopes and zodiac signs.

What is today's horoscope?

Today's horoscope is a daily astrological forecast based on the current planetary positions and their influence on each zodiac sign. It covers themes like love, career, energy, and personal growth. Read your free daily horoscope above for all 12 signs.

What are the 12 zodiac signs?

The 12 zodiac signs are: Aries, Taurus, Gemini, Cancer, Leo, Virgo, Libra, Scorpio, Sagittarius, Capricorn, Aquarius, and Pisces. Each sign belongs to one of four elements: Fire (Aries, Leo, Sagittarius), Earth (Taurus, Virgo, Capricorn), Air (Gemini, Libra, Aquarius), or Water (Cancer, Scorpio, Pisces).

What are the fire signs of the zodiac?

The fire signs are Aries, Leo, and Sagittarius. Fire signs are known for their passion, energy, creativity, and dynamic personalities. They are natural leaders who inspire others with their enthusiasm and courage.

What are the water signs of the zodiac?

The water signs are Cancer, Scorpio, and Pisces. Water signs are known for their emotional depth, intuition, empathy, and strong connection to feelings. They are highly sensitive and often possess psychic or empathic abilities.

Should I read my Sun sign or Rising sign horoscope?

For daily horoscopes, your Sun sign is the most common reference. However, your Rising sign (Ascendant) may feel more accurate for day-to-day events. For a complete picture, read both your Sun and Rising sign forecasts.

What is the most intelligent zodiac sign?

Intelligence manifests differently across signs. Aquarius is often associated with innovative thinking and vision, Virgo with analytical and methodical abilities, and Gemini with quick wit and communication skills. Each sign has unique intellectual strengths.
